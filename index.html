One Shuffle To Rule Them All

Are you tired of never knowing when to stop riffle shuffling? When you get a bad card draw, do you wonder if it was random chance or you just didn't shuffle well enough? Now you can forget all those worries. Use One Shuffle to shuffle your deck just once and get perfect randomization.

Instructions

  1. Take your physical deck of cards and lay them out on a table as shown below. You can select the number of cards in your deck, how many columns you want, and the number of cards per pile so you can fit your deck on your table.
  2. Click or touch anywhere in the box to select the next card, it will be highlighted red.
  3. Pick up that card and put it on your deck.
  4. Keep going until you've collected all the cards. Then you will have a fully randomized deck of cards!
